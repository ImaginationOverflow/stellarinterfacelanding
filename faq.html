About

STELLAR INTERFACE is a rogue-like, permadeath space shooter with randomized perks, providing the player an unique experience every run!

On which platforms will the game be released?

Initially Windows, Linux and Mac via Steam. Console and Mobile support is on the pipeline.

When will it be released?

The master plan is to release Stellar Interface in the final quarter of 2016. We will have an early access available as soon as we get greenlit.

What engine does it use?

Unity.

Who is the team behind it?

Right now the team making StellarInterface is composed by two developers, Diogo Cardoso e Miguel Azevedo, and a designer, Rita Malaquias.
