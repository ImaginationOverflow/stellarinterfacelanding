It's on! A space-shooter meets a rogue-like-ish game, and STELLAR INTERFACE is born.

Run and explore through space, with a permadeath mechanic breathing over your neck! Find and install numerous software-perks which will boost your spacecraft. Well, it might not always boost...

With countless weapons of different types to assemble in your spacecraft, your strategy must change quickly in your deep-space run. And the symbiotic nature of the perks will scale even more the uniqueness of game experience.

Integrating achievements and with it's own "spacencyclopedia", the StellarNet, there will be a lot to collect in the STELLAR INTERFACE's vast universe.

Replay replay and replay the same game with complete different tones each run! What will happen to you, in your own unique apocalyptic cataclysmic transgalactic voyage?

STELLAR INTERFACE will be soon launched in Early Access, and even sooner in Steam's Greenlight. Stay tuned!
